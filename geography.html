Geography

Batac City is a landlocked component city in the province of Ilocos Norte, located in the Ilocos Region (Region I). It has a land area of approximately 161.06 square kilometers, which is about 4.71% of the total area of Ilocos Norte. The city is bounded by several municipalities: Banna, Currimao, Paoay, Pinili, Sarrat, Marcos, and San Nicolas. It is around 17.53 km from Laoag City, the provincial capital, and about 466–470 km from Manila.


The terrain of Batac is mostly flat to rolling, especially around the poblacion (town center), while rural barangays feature more sloping and elevated areas. Like the rest of Ilocos, the city experiences a distinct wet season (May to October) and dry season (November to April).
